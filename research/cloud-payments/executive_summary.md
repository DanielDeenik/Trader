## MOSAIC THEORY FRAMEWORK: HOW TO BUILD INFORMATION EDGE

### The Five-Layer Information Mosaic

**Layer 1: Public Filings & Announcements**
- SEC Edgar (confidential IPO submissions, patent filings)
- Company press releases (customer wins, partnerships)
- Earnings calls (public comparables: Adyen, Klarna post-IPO)

**Layer 2: Alternative Data Signals**
- GitHub: SDK stars, forks, commit frequency, contributor diversity
- Stack Overflow: Q&A volume, answer quality, sentiment
- Job postings: Hiring velocity, roles, geographic expansion

**Layer 3: Regulatory & Compliance**
- FedRAMP certifications (GSA.gov)
- Banking license applications (Federal Reserve, OCC)
- Government contracts (SAM.gov)

**Layer 4: Executive & Organizational**
- LinkedIn: Executive hires/departures, hiring patterns, org changes
- Glassdoor/Blind: Employee sentiment, attrition signals
- Org structure changes (new departments = strategic shifts)

**Layer 5: Comparable Company Analysis**
- Recent IPO multiples (Klarna 11x, Adyen 35x)
- Peer company growth rates (Stripe 34%, Adyen 20%)
- Valuation anchors (secondary market pricing via Carta, EquityZen)

### Assembly Process (12-Week Timeline)

**Weeks 1-2**: Gather baseline data (all public sources)
**Weeks 2-4**: Map alternative data (GitHub, Stack Overflow, job postings)
**Weeks 4-8**: Identify divergences (where consensus vs. signals conflict)
**Weeks 8-12**: Build thesis (mosaic + valuation + catalysts)

**Output**: Investment thesis with 2-3x upside catalyst and 12-36 month timeline
