## FINAL NOTES

**Research Philosophy**: This framework operationalizes mosaic theory (SEC Regulation FD compliant) by synthesizing non-material public signals into material investment conclusions. The advantage accrues to disciplined researchers who detect repricing catalysts 6-18 months before institutional consensus.

**Key Insight**: ESG scoring requires human judgment (60% aspirational, 40% performance). This creates systematic timing lags that enable information arbitrage—similar to traditional security analysis but applied to sustainability/climate metrics.

**Competitive Advantage**: Build proprietary mosaic intelligence before consensus institutional adoption. The window is 12-36 months as regulatory catalysts (CSRD, DoD, EU Green Deal) drive adoption waves.

**Execution Reality**: Success requires disciplined signal monitoring (weekly/monthly/quarterly), not occasional deep dives. The framework is designed for operational integration into existing investment processes.
