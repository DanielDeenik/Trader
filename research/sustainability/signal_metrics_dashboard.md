## CONCLUSION: FROM SIGNALS TO CONVICTION

This dashboard framework operationalizes the mosaic theory approach:

1. **Weekly alerts** catch regulatory/market timing signals
2. **Monthly research** deepens signal understanding (rating divergence, hiring trends)
3. **Quarterly reviews** synthesize mosaic into repricing theses
4. **Annual planning** recalibrates opportunity set and confidence rankings

**Key discipline**: Track signals *before* consensus pricing moves. The arbitrage window closes once institutional capital deploys, ratings update, and analyst consensus shifts. Early signal detection (6-18 months lead) is what converts information into asymmetric returns.

**Operational reality**: Most investors monitor lagging indicators (earnings, rating changes, M&A announcements). Mosaic researchers detect leading indicators (regulatory timelines, corporate commitment announcements, hiring surges, capital flows) that precede consensus repricing. That time advantage is the alpha.
